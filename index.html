Wilderness Festival 2025

31st July - 3rd August 2025

Step into the Wilderness

Join us for an unforgettable weekend of exploration, freedom and adventure. Nestled in the heart of breathtaking Cornbury Park, Wilderness invites you to immerse yourself in a world of creative expression, dazzling entertainment and captivating performances. Escape into nature, go wild swimming in the lake or soak in a hot-tub under the stars.

Every moment has been designed with you in mind. Join us for a weekend that can be as relaxing or as wild as you desire.

Explore

Accommodation

Whether you are looking for the ease of a pre-pitched tent, our dedicated family camping area, or our range of boutique accommodation options for something more luxurious, discover your perfect home for the weekend.

Line up

For thirteen years, we have been bringing the worlds’ greatest artists and DJs, speakers and comics together for a line up like no other. Whether you want belly laughs before dinner or to party into the night, 2025 is set to be the best yet.

Experiences

With an abundance of experiences, you can truly make the weekend your own. From paddle-board-yoga and wild-swimming to poetry writing, forest banquets and cooking workshops with award-winning chefs... the choice is yours.

Everything you need to know...

Early-bird tickets are already on sale so there is no better time to plan your escape. Here’s all the information you need.

Who we are: We are Wilderness Festival. Over a four day weekend we put on a spectacle, showcasing the finest talent there is in the worlds of the arts, music, comedy, dining, outdoors and wellness. But the magic cannot happen without you. Join us and curate your own Wilderness.

When: 31st July – 3rd August 2025

Where: Nestled within the stunning grounds of Cornbury Park in Oxfordshire

Ticket Price: £278
